## Pre Solo Maneuvers

* Both maneuvers are about altering your bank and crab with relation to the wind.
    * When heading downwind, you'll have the greatest bank to avoid overshooting your track
    * When heading crosswind, you'll be crabbed into the wind to keep you from being pushed off your track
    * When heading upwind, you'll have the least bank to avoid undershooting your track

* Circles about a point
    * Start downwind; this will require the greatest bank in your first turn, so you won't exceed 45 degrees in subsequent turns.
* S curves over a road
    * Start downwind, same deal
